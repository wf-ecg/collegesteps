Financial Aid Journey Overview

Find the net price calculator on your school’s website, and consider the results. If there’s a gap between your estimated total cost and your aid award, that gap represents your additional out-of-pocket expenses. If the school’s estimated financial aid award will cover your costs, use the timeline in Step 5 to know your due dates.
